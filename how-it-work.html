3 Steps To Straight Teeth.

No monthly clinic visits, ho hidden costs. Everything delivered straight to your door.

learn more

1. It begins with your smile.

Get a free scan at becomeStudio™, or an Impression Kit to make your teeth impressions at home. Our orthodontists then evaluate your case.

2. Our orthodontists design your treatment.

You get a customised treatment plan and a simulation of your new smile. If you like it, you can proceed to order your invisible braces.

3. You receive your box of invisible braces.

We deliver the full set of invisible braces straight to your door – even if you pay in instalments! And you even get a free set of retainers after treatment.

How invisible braces work.

Invisible braces gradually shift your teeth into an ideal position. By wearing a series of invisible braces for 2 weeks at a time, your teeth move up to 0.3mm per set until ideal alignment is reached.
