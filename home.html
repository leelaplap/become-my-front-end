Your most frequent questions, answered.

WHAT ARE BECOME INVISIBLE BRACES?

Our invisible braces treatment includes several sets of tight-fitting, custom-made clear plastic trays that gradually shift teeth into their desired position.

WHAT ARE BECOME INVISIBLE BRACES MADE FROM?

become invisible braces are made from a durable, breakthrough thermoplastic to bring you comfortable, yet high-performing invisible braces that won’t irritate your cheeks and gums like traditional metal braces often do. Our invisible braces are completely free of harmful BPA, phthalates and parabens.

HOW DO I KNOW IF BECOME INVISIBLE BRACES ARE RIGHT FOR ME?

Our free Smile Assessment and Starter Kit help our orthodontists determine the right treatment for you. The invisible braces system is designed for minor to moderate teeth corrections, including spaces between teeth, crowding, rotations, and some bite corrections.

WHAT IF I HAVE WISDOM TEETH?

If your dentist has recommended removal of wisdom teeth, then please follow that direction. If not, you can purchase our Starter Kit and take your impressions for our orthodontist to review your smile for you. If it is determined that our become invisible braces treatment isn’t right for you, we will refund the cost of your impression kit.
