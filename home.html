How become works.

1. It all begins with that smile of yours

Get a free scan at our becomeStudio™, or buy a Starter Kit to make your teeth impressions at home. Our licensed orthodontists then evaluate your case.

2. Review your treatment plan

We send you your customised treatment plan and a preview of your new smile. If you love your smile preview, you can then proceed to order your invisible braces.

3. Receive your box of invisible braces

We deliver the full set of invisible braces straight to your door - even if you pay in instalments! After completing treatment, you get a complimentary set of retainers.

Are become invisible braces for me?

Invisible braces are not for everyone as they are prescribed on a case-by-case basis. Take our free 30-second smile assessment to see if we can straighten your smile!
